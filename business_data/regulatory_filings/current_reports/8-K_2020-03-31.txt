India South, Brazil South) and are observing deployments for some compute resource types in these regions drop below our typical 99.99&nbsp;percent success rates. </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:ARIAL">We&#146;re actively monitoring performance and usage trends to ensure we&#146;re optimizing services for gamers worldwide. At the same time, we&#146;re taking proactive
